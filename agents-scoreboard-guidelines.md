# Scoreboard Glossary & Guidelines

The scoreboard now defaults to a 12-metric simple grid for "at a glance" clarity.

Here are what the 12 metrics in the grid mean:

1. MINS TODAY
Total minutes you have worked today.

2. LEFT TODAY
Minutes remaining to hit your daily goal.

3. TODAY GOAL
Your target minutes for today.

4. $ TODAY
Total money earned today.

5. $ LEFT TODAY
Money remaining to hit today's daily goal.

6. $ MONTH
Total money earned so far this month.

7. $ LEFT MONTH
Money remaining to hit your ultimate monthly goal.

8. BREAK USED
Coffee break minutes used today out of your 90-minute allowance.

9. MO AVG
Your average minutes hit per day so far this month.

10. REQ TO MIN
The new daily average you must hit going forward to barely reach the minimum (Level 1) goal.

11. REQ TO LVL2
The new daily average you must hit going forward to reach the Growth (Level 2) goal.

12. CURR CALL
Duration of the current active call and the unbanked money earned during it.

## Quick Rules
- Resets happen at midnight (00:00).
- Avail time is only tracked between 9 AM and 11:59 PM.
- If you need the old gamified layout, click "RETURN TO GAMIFIED VIEW" at the bottom of the grid.
